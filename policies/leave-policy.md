# OnlyAI Leave Policy

## 1. Annual Leave
- Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year.
- Leave must be requested at least 1 week in advance.
- Annual leave balance does not roll over to the next calendar year.

## 2. Sick Leave
- Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year.
- A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days.

## 3. Maternity & Paternity Leave
- Maternity Leave: 16 weeks of paid leave.
- Paternity Leave: 2 weeks of paid leave.

## 4. Bereavement Leave
- Employees are entitled to 3 days of paid leave for the loss of an immediate family member.

## 5. Eligibility
- Employees are eligible for paid leave after completing 3 months of continuous service (Probation Period).
